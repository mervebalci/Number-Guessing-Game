    Number-Guessing Game
* Ask the player to guess a mystery number between 0 and 99.
* Allow the player to enter a guess in a textfield by clicking a button.
* Use an if statement to figure out if the number entered is 
    greater than, 
    less than, 
    or the same as the mystery number.
* Display a message that tells the player if they guessed 
    too low, 
    too high, 
    or correct.
